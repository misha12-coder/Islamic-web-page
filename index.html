☽ Hazrat Muhammad ﷺ: Rehmat-ul-Lil-Aalameen

Hazrat Muhammad ﷺ ki paidaish 12 Rabi-ul-Awwal (Aam-ul-Feel) ko Makkah Mukarrama mein hui. Aap ﷺ Allah Ta'ala ke wo azim-ush-shaan Mehboob hain jin ke sadqe Allah ne is poori kainat ko takhleeq kiya, isi liye Aap ﷺ ko 'Mehboob-e-Khuda' kaha jata hai.

Aap ﷺ ki zaat-e-mubaraka mein Allah ne tamam purane Ambiya ki khubiyo ko jama kar diya tha. Aap ﷺ ne na sirf dunya ko tauheed ka rasta dikhaya, balkay apne amli kirdar se sachaai, amanatdari, aur reham-dili ki aisi misaal qayam ki jis ki nazeer nahi milti.

Aap ﷺ ki shafqat sirf insanon tak mahdood na thi, balkay aap parindo aur janwaron ke liye bhi rehmat ban kar aaye. Allah Ta'ala ne Quran-e-Majid mein farmaya ke hum ne Aap ﷺ ko tamam jahanon ke liye 'Rehmat-ul-lil-Aalameen' bana kar bheja hai.

⚔ Ya Ali Shere Khuda (R.A)

Hazrat Ali ibn Abi Talib (R.A) wo azeem shakhsiyat hain jinhein "Baab-ul-Ilm" (Ilm ka Darwaza) kaha jata hai. Aap ki shujaat aur bahaduri ki misaal dunya mein nahi milti. Khyber ka maidan ho ya Badr o Ohad, aap ki talwar ne hamesha haq ka parcham buland kiya.

📖

Irfan-e-Ali

"Zaleel hai wo shakhs jo ilm rakhte hue bhi amal na kare." Aap ki hikmat bhari baatein aaj bhi insaniyat ke liye rasta dikhati hain.

⚔️

Shujaat-e-Ali

Ghazwa-e-Khaybar mein Aap ne us qila ko fatah kiya jis ko poori lashkar fatah na kar saki. Aap ki bahaduri zarb-ul-misal hai.

💎

Zuhd-e-Ali

Khilafat ke bawajood Aap ne sada sada zindagi guzari. Dunya ki taraf rughbat na thi, Aap ka dil hamesha Allah ki taraf jhuka raha.

سَيِّدَةُ نِسَاءِ الْعَالَمِيْنَ

🌸 Hazrat Fatima (R.A) aur Aurat ka Muqam

Islam ne aurat ko jo izzat di hai, uski sab se bari misaal Hazrat Fatima-tuz-Zahra (R.A) hain. Aap ﷺ ne farmaya ke "Fatima mere jigar ka tukra hai." Aap Jannat ki khawateen ki sardar hain.

۱

Seerat-e-Fatima

Aap ki zindagi sabr, qana'at aur haya ka majmua thi. Aap ne dikhaya ke kis tarah ek beti apne baap ki khidmat karti hai aur ek maa apne bachon Hasan o Hussain (R.A) ki tarbiyat karti hai.

۲

Aurat ka Haq

Islam ne aurat ko mard ke barabar roohani darja diya. Use virasat mein haq diya, pasand ki shadi ka haq diya aur taleem ko har aurat par farz qarar diya.
